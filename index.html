ERC Advanced Grant Project (2019-2025)

Welcome to TrafficFluid

TrafficFluid (2019-2025) is an ERC (European Research Council) Advanced Grant. It is one of the 222 proposals (out of 2,052 submitted) funded across all scientific disciplines in the ERC-2018-ADG call. TrafficFluid is the only project funded in Greece. This marks the second ERC Advanced Grant awarded to Prof. Papageorgiou, following TRAMAN21 (2013-2018).
